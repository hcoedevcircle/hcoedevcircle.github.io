Intro

Started by some enthusiastic students of Himalaya College of Engineering about a semester ago, this circle is meant to be a platform for students pursuing studies in Himalya College Of Engineering. We are a flexible community of all levels of people from beginner to advanced-beginner

The Circle

Everyone who want themselves inside the club are club members. There are no any rules to define people in or out of the club. Students should watch the club as opportunity. Students should learn from others, help others, plan an event, or do anything creative and the circle will back them. We will try to implement every creative/innovative ideas (that makes sense to some extent). A normal process of getting some work done on your idea would be explaining your idea and letting other creative folks discuss about it and act accordingly.

Events

We regularly conduct informal meetups where new people discuss about new things. This is our way of sharing. We have also successfully conducted events like Webmaker and Software Freedom Day pre-celebration in the college.

And the Next thing on the list is....

Freshmen's Day

This event will be a way to make First Year Students familier with the HCOE Developers' Circle. This will be a interactive and fun event, where people will learn some introductory but best things. And yes, We also have some useful giveaways. :)

Get invlolved

Join our facebook group. Follow us in twitter. Find us in college. There are lots of ways to get involved and take the maximum out of it.
